Restaurant Description

This lovely restaurant located in the heart of New Jersey is an Italian style restaurant. Menu options are plentiful and the kitchen is willing to concoct almost anything your heart may desire. The concept of 'Farm to Table' is very important to us as we shop from as many local vendors as possible. All ingrediants are ordered from the most reputable vendors in the nation. We belive in quality and quantity. While our prices reflect that sentiment, we believe it is the best way to serve our local customers. Our reputation means a lot to us and any issue our customers may ever have, the managers will personally look into it all while finding an immediate solution to give the best customer service possible. Our head chef is 5-star rated from the top cullinary school not just in the nation, but the world. We also have a brick oven which gives our pizza and other pie specialties a perfect crisp. Come join us for a lovely dining experience or even just order for take out!
